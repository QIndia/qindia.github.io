Causes

QIndia members are driven by these core causes. Pretty much, these are the only requirements... to make a significant impact!

Passion

Passion to create a quantum community in India is what unites us. Passion to learn and educate is what drives us.

Education

Basic access to education should be free. We strongly advocate free education materials.

Kinship

We are only strong as ourselves. But together, we define our own limits. QIndia is a community to collaborate, from taking the first step in quantum education to research projects.

Inclusivity

Mutual respect for each other is at the core of QIndia. We have our own QSafe community to overlook and enforce this. We believe in equity, providing opportunity to fellows from the full spectrum of physical, mental and societal backgrounds. Unity in diversity!
